Visit to a Historical Place. (Cox bazer)


Cox's Bazar is the largest sea beach in the world. Everyone has a fascination for visiting Cox's Bazar. I had never seen the sea before. Therefore, I had much curiosity to see the sea. Last autumn vacation, I with some of my friends went to Cox's Bazar. We went there from Dhaka by a hired bus.

We reached there in the afternoon. We put up in a hotel near the sea beach. In the next morning, we got up from bed early and went to the sea beach. We enjoyed the sunrise. We were amazed at seeing the frequent change of color of the sea. Before we reach there we book a ticket of the Hostel from this website Many tourists were walking here and there. Some of them were bathing in the sea. Some of us also took bath in the sea. We enjoyed the sun set scene. Really, that was a wonderful scene. All these, visiting Cox's Bazar is memorable incident in my life.
